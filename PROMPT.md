## 💡 Tips

- **Same seed = same output.** Use specific seeds to reproduce results.
- **Be specific.** "a watercolor painting of a mountain at sunset" > "mountain"
- **Style words matter.** Mention art style, lighting, camera angle.
- **Negative prompts help.** Use them to exclude unwanted artifacts.
- **Resolution matters.** Stick to 1024×1024 for best quality.
